Translating Data Into Decisions

The gap between having data and using it well is where most companies struggle. Raw numbers don't drive decisions; clear analysis does.

I specialize in bridging the technical and the strategic: building dashboards that executives actually use, translating complex findings into clear recommendations, and helping teams ask better questions of their data.

Whether it's a price-volume bridge that explains margin movement or an operational scorecard that catches problems early, the goal is always the same: visibility that leads to action.
